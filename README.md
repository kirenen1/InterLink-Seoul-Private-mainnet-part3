The Final Mission Before Seoul Private Mainnet.
The journey with InterLink has been an exciting one. Over the past year, I’ve watched the ecosystem grow from an ambitious vision into a global community filled with builders, supporters, and believers working toward the same goal.
Throughout this journey, I have learned more about blockchain, digital assets, and the importance of creating technology that solves real-world problems instead of focusing only on speculation. Every campaign, discussion, and community event has strengthened my confidence in InterLink’s long-term vision.

✅ Building one of the world’s largest payment acceptance networks with 10,000 merchants accepting ITL.
✅ Expanding IBTE into a marketplace where real businesses, real transactions, and revenue-backed digital assets create genuine economic value.
✅ Developing a secure, scalable, and quantum resistant blockchain capable of supporting global adoption.
✅ Reaching 100 corporate treasuries in 2026 to strengthen business participation and expand the InterLink economy.
I believe the Seoul Private Mainnet is not the destination it’s the foundation for the next chapter. My vision is to see InterLink become a trusted blockchain ecosystem where digital assets are used in everyday commerce, businesses thrive, and innovation continues to create opportunities worldwide.
Congratulations to the entire InterLink team and community for everything achieved so far. The best is still ahead, and I am proud to be part of this incredible journey.
